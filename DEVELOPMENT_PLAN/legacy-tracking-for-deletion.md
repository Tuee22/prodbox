**2026-07-11 — Lifecycle control-plane redesign opened operational replacement rows.** Two
current aggregate-suite attempts invalidated the earlier claim that injected one-shot probes,
gateway-hosted control-plane routes, a nominal memory plan, and exit-time restore were sufficient
to make readiness and deployment failure classes unrepresentable. Historical removals remain
completed; this correction does not rewrite their evidence. The new Pending Removal rows below own

path is unreachable and the current revision is deployment-qualified under
[Standard P](development_plan_standards.md#p-deployment-qualification-and-counterexample-closure).
